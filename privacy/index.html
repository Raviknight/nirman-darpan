Privacy

What we collect, what we don't, and your rights.

Nirman Darpan is built with the minimum data collection a civic-transparency product can run on. Plain language; nothing buried.

Visiting the site without signing in is fully anonymous from our side. We do not log visitors.

What we do not collect

  • No tracking pixels — no Facebook Pixel, no Google Analytics, no Hotjar / Mixpanel / anything in that category.
  • No advertising networks. There are no ads on this site.
  • No device fingerprinting, browser fingerprinting, canvas tracking.
  • No precise location data. (The HP map shows district polygons, not your location.)
  • No phone numbers. Sign-in is email-only.
  • No payment data — there is nothing to pay for.

Cookies

We use exactly one cookie — your Appwrite session, set on the Appwrite domain. It expires automatically when you sign out, or after a period of inactivity. We do not use any advertising or tracking cookies.

Your rights (DPDP Act 2023)

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 gives you specific rights. We honour them:

  • Right to access — see everything we hold about you. Email ravikntsh@gmail.com with your account email; you'll get an export within 7 days.
  • Right to correction — fix wrong information. Same email.
  • Right to erasure — delete your account and personal data. Comments and votes you posted publicly can either be removed entirely or detached from your identity (anonymised). You choose; we honour within 7 days. Note that the git commit history is a public record we cannot rewrite — only your personal data is removed.
  • Right to grievance redressal — for complaints. ravikntsh@gmail.com.
  • Right to nominate — designate someone else to exercise rights on your behalf in case you cannot.

If you are dissatisfied with our handling of a request, you can escalate to the Data Protection Board of India once it is operational.

Children

Nirman Darpan does not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. Sign-in requires confirmation of adult status (we will add an explicit check). If you believe a minor has signed up, email us and we will remove the account.

Changes to this policy

Material changes are flagged in the corrections log and at the top of this page with the new "last updated" date. Minor wording fixes are committed silently and visible in the git history.
