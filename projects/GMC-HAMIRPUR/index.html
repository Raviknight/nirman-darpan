Dr. Radhakrishnan Govt. Medical College, Hamirpur

Medical college sanctioned in 2014 and operational from 2018 with 120 MBBS seats. Its permanent ~250-bed campus at Jol Sapper near Nadaun is under construction, with completion targeted for March 2026 after cost escalation to ₹417 cr.
