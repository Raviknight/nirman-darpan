Pathankot–Mandi 4-Lane (NH-154)

Upgradation of the ~219 km Pathankot–Mandi corridor to four lanes, split into multiple packages. Progress is highly uneven — some packages near complete while the Palampur–Padhar stretch was held up for years awaiting a DPR.
