Shongtong Karcham Hydroelectric Project (450 MW)

A 450 MW (3×150 MW) run-of-river project with an underground powerhouse on the Satluj in Kinnaur, upstream of Karcham Wangtoo — an HPPCL flagship long delayed past its 2017 target and now pushed toward a 2026 deadline.
