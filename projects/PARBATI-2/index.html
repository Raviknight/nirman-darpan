Parbati-II Hydroelectric Project (800 MW)

An 800 MW (4×200 MW) run-of-river scheme diverting the Parbati near Pulga through a ~31.5 km headrace tunnel to a powerhouse at Siund in Kullu — one of NHPC’s most delayed projects after severe Himalayan geology, finally commissioned in 2025.
