Pekhubela Solar Power Project (32 MW)

Himachal’s first major solar park — a 32 MW ground-mounted plant over about 49 hectares at Pekhubela in Una, with 82,656 modules expected to generate roughly 6.6 crore units a year. Built in around four months and dedicated in 2024.
