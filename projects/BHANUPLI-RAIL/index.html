Bhanupli–Bilaspur–Beri Broad Gauge Rail

A strategically important 63 km broad-gauge line from Bhanupli (Punjab) to Bilaspur — the first leg of a long-planned rail link toward Leh. Tunnel-heavy and slow-moving on land acquisition.
