Atal Institute of Medical Super-Specialities, Chamiyana

Himachal’s first standalone government super-specialty institute, with around 324 beds across ten super-specialty departments (neurology, cardiology, nephrology, urology, gastroenterology and more), built at Chamiyana on the Shimla outskirts under PMSSY-III.
