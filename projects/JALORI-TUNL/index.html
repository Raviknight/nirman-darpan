Jalori Jot Tunnel (NH-305)

A tunnel beneath the snow-bound Jalori Pass on NH-305 to give year-round connectivity to the inner Seraj/Anni belt of Kullu, sanctioned with a ₹1,452 cr central nod in 2025.
