Koldam Hydroelectric Power Station (800 MW)

An 800 MW (4×200 MW) run-of-river project with a ~167 m rockfill dam on the Sutlej near Barmana in Bilaspur — NTPC’s first hydropower station, generating roughly 3,050 GWh a year.
