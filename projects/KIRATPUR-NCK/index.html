Kiratpur–Nerchowk 4-Lane (NH-21)

Four-laning of 92 km of NH-21 between Kiratpur (Punjab) and Nerchowk (Mandi) — shortens the Chandigarh–Manali route by ~46 km, with the country’s tallest highway viaduct over the Bhakra reservoir.
