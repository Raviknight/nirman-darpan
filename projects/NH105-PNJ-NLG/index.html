Pinjore–Baddi–Nalagarh 4-Lane (NH-105)

Four-laning of the ~36 km Pinjore–Baddi–Nalagarh corridor (17 km in HP) serving the Baddi–Barotiwala–Nalagarh industrial belt. Stalled for about a year after the original contractor quit citing land and utility-shifting delays, then revived with a fresh ₹598 cr clearance.
