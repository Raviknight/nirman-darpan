Bajoli Holi Hydroelectric Project (180 MW)

A 180 MW (3×60 MW) run-of-river-with-pondage plant on the upper Ravi near Holi in Chamba, with a 16+ km headrace tunnel — commissioned in 2022 after a multi-year delay and supplying green power including to Delhi airport.
