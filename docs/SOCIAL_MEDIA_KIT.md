## Bios

### Short (Twitter / Bluesky — 160 chars)

> Independent civic-transparency tracker for public works in Himachal Pradesh. 32 projects, all 12 districts. नागरिक के लिए, नागरिक का।

### Medium (LinkedIn tagline — 220 chars)

> Independent, citation-backed tracker for public-works projects across Himachal Pradesh. Status, budgets, accountability records, citizen voices — in one place, on the public record.

### Long (LinkedIn About — ~500 words)

> **Nirman Darpan / निर्माण दर्पण** is an independent civic-transparency project covering public-works infrastructure in Himachal Pradesh. We collect — in one place, for one state — the records, status, budgets, accountability flags and public conversation around every major project the state and centre are running here.
>
> **What's in it.** 32 projects across all 12 districts: highways, hydropower, tunnels, hospitals, rail, smart-city schemes, water supply, schools, disaster restoration. Each project carries the data citizens, journalists and officials should be able to see at a glance — awarded by, contractor, owning department, named leads, milestones, sanctioned outlay, spent-to-date, accountable parties, press mentions in English and Hindi, and (in development) verified resident voices.
>
> **The principles.** Every claim links to its source. No automated sentiment scoring — read the headline yourself. Verified-identity comments only — no anonymous astroturfing. The git commit history is the project's audit log — every change to every record is permanently traceable. Methodology, corrections log, privacy policy, funding sources, code of conduct — all public.
>
> **How it's funded.** Self-funded by the founder. No advertising, no government grant, no contractor sponsorship, no party money. Cost transparency is on the funding page.
>
> **Built by.** Ravi Sharma, independent technologist and HP resident, with the Claude AI assistant as a coding collaborator. All editorial decisions are human.
>
> Open source (MIT). Contact: ravikntsh@gmail.com.
